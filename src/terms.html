Privacy

Cookies

This site uses functional cookies and local storage. That means they're used to get this stuff to work. What they are not used for, is to track you. I just don't have time :)

Personal details

No personal details are collected by iKog-js. If you use the Dropbox feature, iKog-js will have read and write access to the ikog-js app folder. It has no access to any other folders.

Terms of service

The rules are pretty straightforward and are laid out below.

Content

Your tasks are either stored locally on your PC using your browser's local storage, or they are stored using the Dropbox service. Besides that, the data is not stored anywhere else.

Security

iKog-js provides a method of encrypting your entries; the encryption used is AES-256. The password used to encrypt the information is not stored by iKog-js. If you lose it, you've probably lost the information. There is no way of getting it back for you, so make sure your keep your passwords safe.
